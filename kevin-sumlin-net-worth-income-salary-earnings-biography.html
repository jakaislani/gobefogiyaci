Kevin Sumlin Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Kevin Warren Sumlin (born August 3, 1964) is a former American football player and head coach who now resides in Florida. He is now the head football coach at Texas A&M University, a job he took on in December 2011 after being offered it. Previously, from 2007 to 2011, Sumlin served as the head football coach at the University of Houston.
