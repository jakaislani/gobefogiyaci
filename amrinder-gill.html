Amrinder Gill is a Canadian singer, actor, film producer and songwriter who predominantly compose Punjabi-language movies as well as music. He has been elected for 10th PTC Punjabi Filmfare Awards, conquering 6th comprising off three for the Best Actor and two for the Best Playback singer. Amrinder has been elected for 5th Filmfare Awards, conquering two for the Best Playback singer and Best Actor, amongst other awards.

Amrinder Gill composed his first song for the Jalandhar Doordarshan show called “Kala Doria”. He got fame with his song titled “Paigam”, pursued by songs such as Madhaania, Daru, and Khedan De Din together with renowned singer Sunidhi Chauhan, and numerous others such as Dildarian and Mel Kara De. In the year 2012, his music album titled Judaa won a Brit Asian Music Awards for the best album. In the year 2014, Amrinder came up with Judaa 2 sequel. His last track “Supna” was released in the year 2015, since that time, he has released only movie singles.

In the year 2013, Amrinder Gill co-originated a Punjabi show business company called “Rhythm Boyz Entertainment”. In 2009, he initiated his career in acting with a supporting role with the movie titled Munde UK De. After that, he starred as lead in movies like Taur Mittran Di, Ik Kudi Punjab Di and numerous others. Lastly, Amrinder got immense success with movies like Goreyan Nu Daffa Karo and Daddy Cool Munde Fool. Later, movies such as Love Punjab, Angrej, Golak Bugni Bank Te Batua, Ashke and Lahoriye received numerous awards at different award ceremonies.

Amrinder Gill was born in Amritsar, India on 11th May 1976. He is a former student of Guru Nanak Dev University. All through his college days, he used to dance on Bhangra songs as a supporting actor for renowned personalities such as Sarbjit Cheema.  Amrinder’s recordings comprising off Daru, Paigam, Khedan De Din, Madhaania alongside Sunidhi Chauhan and numerous others such as Dildarian and Mel Kara De. His musical album titled Judaa was honored with Brit Asia Music Award for the best album.

His last song track titled Supna was also a big hit on Punjabi music chart. From that time, he has released tracks for Punjabi movies only. His songs titled Kurta Suha from the movie Angrej, “Akhar” from the movie Lahoriye, “Heerey” from the movie Love Punjab, and “Aisi Taisi” from the movie Golak Bugni Bank Te Batua has been elected for numerous awards.
