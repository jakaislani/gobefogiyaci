NXTs Chad Lail And Chris Dijak Receive New Ring Names

In a new photo gallery showcasing every WWE’s Performance Center recruits, two NXT Superstars have received new names.

Chad Lail is named as Jaxson Ryker in the gallery and Chris Dijak is featured in the gallery as Dominik Dijakovic.

Dominik Dijakovic wrestled as Donovan Dijak in Ring of Honor and was using his real name Chris Dijak in NXT since joining WWE last year.

Jaxson Ryker on the other hand, was known as Gunner in Impact and is a former TNA Television Champion and one-time World Tag Team Champion. He signed with WWE last year in May and used his real name, Chad Lail.
