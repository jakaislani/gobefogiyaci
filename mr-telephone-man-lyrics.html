Mr. Telephone Man Lyrics

Mr. Telephone Man is a classic R&B hit song by the American boy band New Edition.

The track was released in November 1984 as part of the group’s self-titled album and was produced by Ray Parker Jr.

The song, which was written by Parker Jr. along with Glenn Barbee and Ollie E. Brown, became a huge success, reaching No. 12 on the US Billboard Hot 100 chart and topping the US Billboard R&B chart.
