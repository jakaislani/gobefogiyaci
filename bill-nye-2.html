Bill Nye aka William Sanford Nye is an American mechanical engineer, science communicator and television presenter. As of 2022, Bill Nye’s net worth is $8 million. He is popularly known for being the host of the popular science television show named Bill Nye the Science Guy.

He is also popularly known for his appearance in the popular Netflix show titled Bill Nye Saves the World and for his several appearances in media as a science educator. He is also known for his invention of the hydraulic resonance suppressor tube.

Bill Nye Wiki/Biography

Born on 27 November 1955, Bill Nye’s age is 66 Years Old as of 2022. He was born and raised in a well-settled Christian family from Washington D.C., United States. He holds an American nationality and has his belief in Christian religion.
