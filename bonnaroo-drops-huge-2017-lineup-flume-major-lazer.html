Bonnaroo Drops Huge 2017 Lineup With Flume, Major Lazer And More

Recommended Videos

Bonnaroo Music + Arts Festival has just revealed their 2017 lineup, revealing a massive talent roster for their upcoming event. Entering into their sixteenth annual outing, Bonnaroo is well established at this point as one of the biggest and best stateside music festivals, and their latest lineup reveal only cements that fact further.

Bonnaroo has confirmed a vast array of top notch dance and electronic acts for their 2017 edition, led by world class DJs like Flume, Major Lazer, Marshmello, Borgore, Getter, Snails, Ookay, NGHTMRE, REZZ and more. Outside of the realm of EDM, the new lineup sports a diverse selection of genres, ranging from rap to pop and rock. U2 and Red Hot Chili Peppers top the lineup on the rock front, while enticing pop acts like Lorde, Tove Lo and The Weeknd are sure to draw in the crowds.

With festival season inching closer, a number of the majors like Coachella have begun to reveal their plans for 2017. There’s still plenty of lineups that have yet to drop, but Bonnaroo is already shaping up to be one of the top festivals of the year.
