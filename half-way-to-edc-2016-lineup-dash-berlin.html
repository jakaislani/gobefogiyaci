EXCLUSIVE: Insomniac Reveals Halfway To EDC 2016 Lineup

Recommended Videos

One of the most magical things about Electric Daisy Carnival Weekend is how it transforms the Las Vegas Strip into a kandi kid’s paradise – and thankfully, you don’t even have to wait all the way until the festival takes place to experience it. For the fifth consecutive year, Insomniac Events promises to temporarily satisfy your desire to stand under the electric sky with Halfway to EDC, a celebration of the celebration itself which will take place at the Marquee Dayclub Dome on February 13th-14th.

Ravers of all kinds will overrun the The Cosmopolitan of Las Vegas during this two-day affair, which promises to be a wild weekend for anyone who’s lucky enough to attend. And now, to heighten the excitement even more, We Got This Covered is thrilled to be able to exclusively premiere to you the official lineup for the event.

Comprised of artists from all over the EDM spectrum, performances from the likes of Dash Berlin, Jauz and SNBRN ensure that the lineup will provide incentive for fans of all genres to make the trip – but for our money, Lost Kings’ set alone is a good enough reason to attend, as the duo appear poised to dominate in 2016 and have promised us a ton of new music over the coming months.
