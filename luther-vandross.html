Biography

Luther Vandross was born in New York City, NY on April 20, 1951. R&B and soul singer-songwriter who recorded “Dance With My Father” in 2003, as well as “Never Too Much” and “Always and Forever.” He led the group Change in the late 1970s. He was a background singer for Stevie Wonder on the track “Part Time Lovers.” He played at the Apollo Theater as part of the group Shades of Jade while he was in high school. Before dropping out to pursue music full-time, Vandross was a student at Western Michigan University.
